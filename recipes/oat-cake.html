Oat cake

Super healthy oat cake!

This is a quick cake that's hearty enough to serve for breakfast or brunch. It can be made in less than a hour!

Ingredients

  • Two eggs
  • One cup of oat flour
  • Two bananas
  • 150 cc. of milk
  • 150 cc. of sunflower oil
  • A drizzle of vanilla extract
  • A teaspoon of baking powder
  • Stevia or sugar

Recipe instructions

  1. Whisk the eggs.
  2. Add vanilla extract, oil, and milk to the eggs and combine.
  3. Mix baking powder into the oat flour and incorporate it into the mixture.
  4. Grease and flour a baking dish.
  5. Pour the mixture into the dish and bake at medium heat.
  6. Once it's ready, remove and enjoy.
